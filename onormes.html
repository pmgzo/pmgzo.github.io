O-Normes

O-Normes is an app for profesionnals who are checking accessibility in public building. We built this project in a group of 4 people, with a support on Android, IOS, and the browser. I was the only one dedicated in the IOS app. This project has been made by iterating with professionals that work in this domain. We iterated with them for around 1 year and a half


This is the home page where we directly have the possibility to:


  • Perform a diagnostic
  • Access performed diagnostic
  • Send directly a feedback to our team, for bugs and feature suggestions
  • Download the last list of checking rules needed to perform a diagnostic

Indeed we are also able to use the app offline, this is why we have the ability to download checking rules

As stated above, checking rules are organized by steps. The accessibility expert can then organize the checking rules order according to the building.


At each checking rules the accessibility expert has the opportunity to verify, note and even taking photos for its report


At any time, the accessibility expert can access all the verified rules, and edit them before publish it on the server


One of the main features of the app is the possibility to use the IPhone's LiDAR (available from IPhone 12 pro).


To take measures which is something that experts frequently do, during audit.


And last but not least, the possibility, to take, retake and validate pictures during checking.

Tools

  • Swift, SwiftUI, Testflight, AVFoundation
