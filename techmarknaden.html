Project

Techmarknaden.se

AnalyticsDataSwedenTech Market

Techmarknaden.se enables developers to study the Tech job market in Sweden. It provides an analytical overview of what the market desires in terms of skills, when to expect companies to publish job posts throughout the year, and much more.

Last version (V3)

This is a demo of the last version of the app. The project is now deployed and can be accessed at techmarknaden.se . We are putting a new infrastructure in place for more affordable costs.

A quick presentation of the v2 version of the app. From data provided by Arbetsförmedlingen (the Swedish employment agency), job posts from 2023 to mid-2025 were retrieved.
