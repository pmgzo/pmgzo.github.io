Söka jobb

Söka jobb enables developers to study the Tech job market in Sweden. And have an overview of what the market desires in term of skills, when to expect a company to publish job posts throughout the year etc...

It proposes more specifically an analytical overview of the Tech job market.

Last version (V2)

Or more rencently this is a quick presentation of the last version of the app.



From data provided by Arbetsförmedlingen (the swedish employment agency). I retrieved job posts from 2023 to mid 2025.


Some numbers:

Reference 498 soft & tech skills.

Soft skills includes languages (swedish, english, german etc...), organization methodologies (AGILE: KANBAN, SCRUM)

Tech skills includes different types:
  • programming languages,
  • programming paradigm (OOP),
  • approach of programming (TDD, DDD),
  • libraries (React, beautifulSoup4, tensorflow),
  • specific technologies (smart contracts, websockets),
  • protocol (wireless, bluetooth, SSL/TLS)
  • frameworks (NestJS, NextJS, Django)
  • etc...


Retrieved 34766 job posts in total from 2557 companies

However among those job posts, 4800 have no skills attributed. The reason is that some job posts doesn't go into the detail of the job and give you only contact or a link to another website

In a near future I aim to cover in addition to developers the others expertises in the Tech industry such as Cloud, Cybersecurity, Data, AI etc...
