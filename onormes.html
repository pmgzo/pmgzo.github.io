Project

O-Normes

iOSAccessibilitySwiftMobileLiDAR

An accessibility auditing app for professionals who inspect public buildings. Built as a team of 4, with support for iOS, Android and browser. I was solely responsible for the iOS app. The project was developed iteratively with domain experts over 1.5 years.

🏛️

Context

O-Normes is designed for accessibility consultants who conduct on-site audits of public buildings. The app guides them through structured checklists, captures evidence, and generates reports — all from a mobile device.

Key features

📶

Offline support

The app works fully offline. Accessibility experts can download all checking rules locally before heading to a building — no connectivity required on-site.

🔀

Flexible rule ordering

Checking rules are organised by steps. Experts can reorder them to match the physical layout of the building they're auditing.

📝

Rich per-rule annotations

At each checking rule, the expert can verify, add notes, and attach photos — all consolidated into a final exportable report.

📐

LiDAR measurements

Using the iPhone's LiDAR sensor (iPhone 12 Pro and above), experts can take precise AR-assisted measurements directly inside the app.

📸

Photo management

Full photo capture workflow per checking rule — take, retake, and validate pictures inline during the audit session.
